## Understanding Agentic AI

### What is Agentic AI?

Think of agentic AI as the self-driving car of the AI world. Unlike traditional AI, which often needs human intervention, agentic AI operates autonomously. It perceives its environment, reasons, and acts independently to achieve goals. This autonomy allows it to handle dynamic environments and adapt based on new information.

### The Shift from Traditional AI

Traditional AI excels at narrow tasks like image recognition but struggles with complex, dynamic tasks. Agentic AI breaks these barriers, managing intricate processes without constant oversight. It's like moving from a manual to an automatic gearbox—less hands-on management, more strategic focus.
