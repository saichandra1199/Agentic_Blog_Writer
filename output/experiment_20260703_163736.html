Understanding Agentic AI

Definition and Differentiation

What is agentic AI? Unlike traditional AI that needs human input, agentic AI operates independently. It makes decisions and acts autonomously, adapting in real-time to new data and environments. This contrasts with assistive AI, which supports human decisions but doesn't act alone. Think of agentic AI as your business's autopilot—constantly learning, optimizing, and executing without manual input.
